WHO I AM

I move through design and life, with curiosity and care. I’m drawn to things that feel real, the kind you can’t fake: honest materials, genuine people, and work that says something quietly but with conviction. I put my heart into what I make, always learning, always listening, trying to build not just structures but meaning. I don’t rush; I refine. I want my work to breathe, to connect people, place, and purpose in a way that feels both grounded and alive.

WHAT I WANT

I want to work with a team that values real collaboration where ideas move both ways and curiosity is part of the culture. I’m drawn to mentors who teach with generosity but remain open to learning, and to projects that challenge me to think deeply and design thoughtfully. Above all, I hope my work improves people’s lives and contributes positively to the world, creating spaces that are meaningful, humane, and lasting.

WHAT I CAN DO

My youth and passion for creativity drive me to approach every architectural challenge with a fresh, open perspective. I’m deeply committed to learning and refining my craft, balancing technical precision with a sensitivity to the emotional impact of design. With a strong work ethic and dependable character, I contribute thoughtfully and collaboratively to every team, ensuring that each project is completed with care and purpose.
